I didn't plan to love you. It wasn't dramatic. It wasn't loud.

It was quiet. Like rain at night. Like a song only I could hear.

Somewhere between your laugh and the way you say my name, my heart decided.

Love You!

This is not a request. Not a persuasion. Not a "please choose me."

Love is not something you owe anyone.

If your heart doesn't beat the same way, that is okay.

I would rather lose you honestly than keep you unfairly.

Maybe in another universe,
we met at the right time.
With the right hearts.
Without fear.
And maybe there,
you loved me too.
But in this one,
I will simply thank you
for existing.

Some loves are meant to be felt, not kept.
